# Black-Scholes-Merton
Closed form solution of the Black-Scholes-Merton formula for various types of **european** options. 


    - Vanilla Options 
    - Binary Cash or nothing
    - Binary Asset or nothing
    - Barriers
        - Knock-in
            - Up and In
            - Down and In
        - Knock-out
            - Up and Out
            - Down and out
