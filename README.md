# Black-Scholes-Merton
Closed form solution of the Black-Scholes-Merton formula for various types of **european Call/Put** options. 


    - Vanilla:
        - Greeks:
            - Delta
            - Gamma
            - Tau
            - Rho
            - Vega

    - Binary Cash or nothing
        - Greeks:
            - Delta
            - Gamma
            - Tau
            - Rho
            - Vega

    - Binary Asset or nothing
        - Greeks:
            - Delta
            - Gamma
            - Tau
            - Rho
            - Vega
            
    - Barriers
        - Knock-in
            - Up and In
            - Down and In
        - Knock-out
            - Up and Out
            - Down and out
